# Scaling Laws (Kaplan et al., 2020)

Loss scales as a power law with model size, dataset size, and compute.

## Key Findings
- Larger models are more sample-efficient
- Performance is predictable from scale
- Compute-optimal: balance model size and data

Relevant to both [[GPT]] and [[BERT]] scaling decisions.
