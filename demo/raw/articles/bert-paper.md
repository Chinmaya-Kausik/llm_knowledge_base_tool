# BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers

Notes on the [[BERT]] paper by Devlin et al.

## Pre-training Objectives
- Masked Language Modeling (15% of tokens)
- Next Sentence Prediction

## Key Results
- SOTA on 11 NLP tasks at time of publication
- Showed that bidirectional context matters
