Let me check the current state to see what's most impactful.Here's where the project stands and what I'd suggest:

**Current state:** Sorting task + EN→DE translation both working with word-level tokenizer, greedy decoding, and warmup LR schedule.

**Two planned next steps:**

1. **BPE tokenizer** — Higher impact. The word-level tokenizer (vocab capped at 8K) can't handle unseen words or morphologically rich German well. BPE would significantly improve translation quality. You'd modify `tokenizer.py` to learn merge rules, update `translation_data.py` to use it, and adjust `config.yaml`.

2. **Beam search decoding** — Easier to implement. Add a `beam_search()` function alongside the existing greedy decode in `model.py` or a new `decode.py`. Wire it into eval in `train_translation.py`. Typically gives a few BLEU points for free.

**My recommendation:** BPE first — it's the bigger bottleneck for translation quality and more educational. Beam search is a nice follow-up that's relatively quick to add after.
