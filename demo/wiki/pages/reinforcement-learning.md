# Reinforcement Learning

An agent learns by interacting with an environment, receiving rewards.

## Key Algorithms
- Policy Gradients (REINFORCE)
- PPO (Proximal Policy Optimization) — used for RLHF in language models
- DQN — deep Q-networks

## Connection to LLMs
RLHF uses PPO to align language models ([[GPT]], Claude) with human preferences.
